The Team

We at the Artling are passionate about Art and we aim to provide the best service possible to our clients. We’re always looking out for talented people, join our team!

Talenia Phua Gajardo

CEO / Founder

Talenia is the CEO of The Artling. Half Singaporean and half Chilean, Talenia grew up in Singapore, was educated in Australia and London, where she spent almost a decade, and also worked for several years at architect Zaha Hadid’s eponymous firm, before founding her own architecture and design practice. After returning to Singapore in 2013, she founded The Artling to create an aggregated online platform for discovering contemporary art.

Talenia’s life passions are intertwined with the world of contemporary art, architecture and design. She has served as design judge for the Dezeen Awards, was on Hong Kong’s Design Trust benefit committee and was a Milken Institute Young Leader Circle member. She has spoken at numerous international conferences and has been part of panels for UBS, DBS, The Milken Institute, Frieze, and more.

She is a Loke Cheng-Kim Foundation scholar and received her BA (Hons) Architecture from Central Saint Martin’s in London.

Kim Tay

Gallery Director / Co-Founder

Kim is the Co-Founder and Gallery Director of The Artling. She joined the company in 2014 after spending a number of years in the United States and Russia. She oversees The Artling’s online inventory and leads the company’s various consultancy projects across the globe, sourcing and commissioning artworks for clients from Google to Ritz Carlton.

Kim’s expertise in art collecting, Southeast Asia’s regional art scenes, and the crucial synergies between art and technology has been shared on panels and talks across Asia for Hermes, HSBC, Credit Suisse and more.

The Singapore native holds a degree in Art History and Government from Georgetown University in Washington, DC.

Chua Xuan Yi

Art Consultant

Xuan Yi joined The Artling in 2023 with almost 20 years of experience as an Interior Design Consultant. She has completed numerous projects that includes luxury hotels, high-end residences, and commercial spaces.

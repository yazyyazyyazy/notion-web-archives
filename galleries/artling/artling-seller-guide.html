About The Artling

The Artling is the world’s leading resource for Asian Contemporary Art & Design. We have established ourselves as a reputable online gallery, working with some of the top galleries and artists in the region helping them sell art online to a world of art lovers and collectors.

We make it easy for you to sell your original work to a global audience through our online gallery and art advisory service. We also help you with the promotion and branding of your artwork.

Why sell your artwork on The Artling?

Benefits

▪ Increase your online exposure - reach a global audience actively looking to buy art online

▪ Listing is free – we just charge a commission on artworks sold

▪ It allows you to be part of a leading platform that showcases the best of Asian contemporary art and design

Features

▪ Exclusive artist features on The Artling Artzine

▪ Promotion to our extensive social media following on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter

▪ An offline avenue for sales through inclusion in our Art Advisory proposals to private and corporate clients

▪ The Artling team on hand to support you every step of the way!

How to Price your Artwork

The pricing of your work is a very important aspect of your career as an artist. You should be able to clearly explain to anyone asking the reason for your artworks’ pricing. 

Here are some things to consider: 

▪ Look at your peers’ artworks in similar media and see how your prices compare. 

▪ Have your works been included in exhibitions before? 

▪ Have you won art awards or prizes recently? 

▪ Consider the cost of your materials. 

▪ Is your artwork a Unique Work or an Editioned Work?

▪ Lastly, make sure you include The Artling’s commission in the price you upload to the website.

If you’re still unsure, get in touch with our team here who can provide further advice.

Using Keywords

When selling art online, it’s important to tell the story behind your work. What inspired you to create the piece? Include details on where the work has been exhibited previously. All this information helps the buyer understand more about you, the artist, as well as the work they are considering.

What Happens When I Sell My Item?

For Artists, Designers & Galleries

1. Customer Registers Interest in Your Item:
You will receive an automatic email notifying you about the customer's request for the shipping cost of your item to their address

2. Provide Shipping Cost:
You will need to provide an accurate shipping cost (with transit insurance) from the item location to the buyer's address. The customer will then decide if they would like to proceed with their purchase.

3. Condition Check: 
If the customer confirms their purchase, you will be notified through email to submit the images of the item before and after packing for our condition check in your Seller Portal.

4. Ship the Item to the Customer's Address:
After the condition check is completed, you will be notified via email and will then need to ship the item and the Certificate of Authenticity to the customer's address.

Please use a professional art handler or recognised local or international courier to ship the item to their address, and submit the shipment's tracking details through your Seller Portal.

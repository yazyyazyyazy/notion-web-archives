About The Artling

Transforming spaces through art and design

The Artling is an international Art and Design consultancy and online gallery focused on sourcing and highlighting the best of contemporary art and design.

  • Our online platform, theArtling.com, lists thousands of artists from around the world, with new artworks and design items added to the site daily.

  • The Artling offers consultancy services to both private and corporate clients, working with private collectors and with interior designers, architects and property developers.

  • Sourcing from both emerging and established artists, The Artling provides end-to-end advisory service, from developing art themes and narratives, concept design development, and the commissioning of artworks to project and site specifications.
